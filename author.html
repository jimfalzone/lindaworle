About The Author

Meet Linda Worle, a literary virtuoso whose quill effortlessly brings the magic of gardens to life on every page. With a profound affinity for both nature's beauty and the boundless curiosity of young minds, Linda creates captivating tales that bloom in the hearts of readers young and old. Residing amidst the lush landscapes of Florida with her husband Tom, a kindred spirit, she draws inspiration from her surroundings, crafting garden-themed narratives that whisk readers away on imaginative journeys. With each meticulously woven word, Linda plants seeds of wonder, nurturing a love for gardens, growth, and the limitless possibilities of storytelling.
